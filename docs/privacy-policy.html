Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2024

At Greek Alphabets AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Text input for translations and learning purposes
  • Camera images for text recognition (processed locally on your device)
  • Favorites and learning preferences you save within the app

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Our app may automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics to improve our services
  • Crash reports and error logs for debugging purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide language learning features, translations, and text recognition
  • Improvement: To enhance app functionality and user experience
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our app and improve our services

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data, including favorites and preferences, is stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync it.

3.2 Cloud Services

When using AI-powered features, your text input may be sent to third-party AI services for processing. We ensure that:

  • Data is transmitted securely using encryption
  • We only send necessary information for the requested service
  • We work with reputable service providers who comply with privacy regulations

3.3 Camera and Image Data

Images captured for text recognition are processed locally on your device whenever possible. If cloud processing is required, images are processed and not stored permanently.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • AI Translation Services: For providing translation and language learning assistance
  • ML Kit (Google): For text recognition and image processing
  • Analytics Services: To understand app usage and improve performance

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access your stored data within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your favorites and preferences at any time
  • Opt-out: You can disable certain features that require data collection
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Users

If you are using our app from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.
